Answers to your last questions

How much time do I really need to dedicate to the program each week?

The program is designed for leaders' schedules. Plan for 1 to 2 hours per week. Formats are short and actionable. The idea is not to add more work, but to offer you solutions that bring clarity from the very first week.

I'm not a technical expert, will I be able to keep up?

Absolutely. This program is not for technicians, it is for decision-makers. We don't talk about code, but about strategy, governance, and impact. You will learn to steer AI and make it a leadership lever, not to become an engineer.

The 4,000 euro investment is significant. How do I justify it?

It's a direct investment in your leadership skills for the decade ahead. Beyond the content, you invest in a personalized journey, access to a network of experts and peers, and a measurable transformation. It's much more than training, it's an accelerator.

Is the program personalized or the same for everyone?

The journey is personalized from day one. Everything starts with an individual diagnostic to understand your challenges. Then, the content and deliverables are adapted based on your profile and your organization's priorities. You benefit from the strength of the collective, without ever being in a generic mold.

No worries. All sessions are recorded and available as replays, accessible 24/7. The private community (on WhatsApp) also allows you to ask your questions at any time. The program is 100% flexible.
