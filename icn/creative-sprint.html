1

Smart Process & Resolution

🏆 Tokyo Singapour

Simplify processes upstream (KISS) + resolve complaints downstream (CLOSE)

+

🔗 Why This Pairing

Tokyo wants to simplify internal processes with a KISS approach — they talk about an "obstacle course" for ATM competition students. Singapour built CLOSE, a 5-step system to structure complaints with AI (368 complaints → 7 categories). Both attack the same problem from two ends: simplify upstream (Tokyo) and resolve downstream (Singapour). Together, it's an end-to-end process streamlining project.

🎯 Tokyo — KISS

Keep It Simple and Stupid: shorten processes, "quick start" spirit, major ATM competition for students to reimagine the school. Focus on student experience and sense of belonging.

🎯 Singapour — CLOSE

Capture, Label, Own, Solve, Explain. Collect complaints, structure them with AI into 7 categories, resolve and communicate. Quick fixes + long-term structural improvement.

🅍 Natural Bridges

Tokyo simplifies upstream, Singapour resolves downstream. Together they create a complete process improvement loop: prevention + resolution.

2

ICN Connected

Buenos Aires Vancouver

Break down inter-department silos — digital platform + skills directory

+

🔗 Why This Pairing

Buenos Aires talks about "silos" and proposes a digital platform + "Walk in my shoes" between departments. Vancouver talks about "who does what" and proposes expertise exchange groups + skills database. Same diagnosis (we don't know each other between departments), same remedy (build bridges). Buenos Aires brings the immersive aspect, Vancouver brings the information structure. The most natural pairing.

🎯 Buenos Aires

Break silos with a centralized digital exchange space, "Live My Life" immersive program, and wow-factor onboarding. Less friction, faster decisions, multiplied collective energy.

🎯 Vancouver

Inter-expert exchange groups, "who does what" directory, internal newsletters. Experts finally consulted, information flowing, informed decisions.

🅍 Natural Bridges

Buenos Aires brings human immersion (Walk in my shoes), Vancouver brings information architecture (skills directory). Together: people know each other AND know who does what.

3

The Stable — Feedback Culture

🏆 Helsinki Dublin

F1 metaphor: recognition + constructive tension resolution

+

🔗 Why This Pairing

Helsinki uses the F1 stable metaphor — "Walk in my shoes", recognition, horizontality. Dublin proposes a "constructive conflict culture" with neutral facilitators. Both projects are on the same ground: transforming how people talk to each other at ICN. Helsinki through recognition and mutual support, Dublin through tension resolution. Direct complementarity.

🎯 Helsinki — The Stable

F1 metaphor on 3 axes: The Gears (horizontality), The Stable (recognition of ALL tasks, salary revaluation), The Pilots ("Live my challenge", co-piloting, transparency). Goal: TOP 10.

🎯 Dublin — Constructive Conflict

Regular "conflict" meetings with neutral facilitators, cross-functional and horizontal meetings. Reduction of burnout and turnover, increased performance and satisfaction.

🅍 Natural Bridges

Helsinki provides the recognition framework (positive), Dublin provides the tension resolution framework (constructive). Together: a complete feedback culture where people feel valued AND heard.

ATM in Action

🏆 Montréal Sydney

Make ATM tangible daily — identity + concrete initiatives

+

🔗 Why This Pairing

Montréal won with a project centered on ATM identity. Sydney proposes an ATM task force + summer artist residency + student inclusion in projects. Same ambition to make ATM tangible daily — Montréal provides the identity framework, Sydney brings concrete initiatives (residency, task force). Logical merger.

🎯 Montréal

Concrete action plan for each campus articulated around ATM skills. Connect ATM to everyone's daily missions. Student engagement, visibility for employers, pride of belonging.

🎯 Sydney

Task force of students + staff + teachers + decision-makers + alumni. Summer artist residency on campuses. Influence, external relations, press and media visibility.

🅍 Natural Bridges

Montréal defines what ATM means (identity), Sydney makes it happen (residency, task force). Together: ATM has a clear definition AND visible actions.

5

Campus Magnets

🏆 Barcelona Le Cap

Distinctive campus identity (Harry Potter) + shared values foundation (SOCLE)

+

🔗 Why This Pairing

Barcelona imagined an identity hub per campus (Nancy = finance, Paris = luxury, Berlin = tech) with a constituent assembly — "Harry Potter" for houses. Le Cap proposes SOCLE (shared values) + an "ICN Welcome" pack for newcomers. Barcelona differentiates campuses, Le Cap unifies them through shared foundations. The tension between both is productive: each campus has its color AND shares common values.

🎯 Barcelona — Harry Potter Houses

Nancy = finance hub (Luxembourg), Paris = luxury/hospitality hub, Berlin = entrepreneurship/tech hub. Each hub embodies ATM uniquely. Constituent assembly + school vote.

🎯 Le Cap — SOCLE

FOUNDATION language (Solidarity, Originality, Creativity, Leadership, Excellence) + concrete ATM definition + "ICN Welcome" package: pitch, welcome day, tutoring.

🅍 Natural Bridges

Barcelona differentiates (each campus gets its color), Le Cap unifies (shared values). The productive tension: identity AND belonging. Each campus is unique AND part of one ICN.
